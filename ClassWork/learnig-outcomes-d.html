Background image(CSS)

[background-image:url(url)]

Propperties:
[background-repeat:] repeat , repeat-x , repeat-y , no-repeat , space , round.
[background-atachment:] scroll , fixed , local
[background-position:] centre , left top , left centre , left bottom , right top
[background-clip:] content-box ,padding-box , border-box .
[background-size:] auto(image origional size ) , cover ,contain(image is fully visible width & height)

Border Elements

[border: 2px solid green] (width , color , style In any Order)

Border types: dotted , dashed , solid , doouble , groove , ridge , inset , outset

Note: You can also use border images [border-image:url(url)]

Text Shadow

Add text shadowing horizontally or vertically

[text-shadow: 1px , 1px , 2px ,red] (offset-x | offset-y | blur-radius | color)
blur radius creates a blur effect by spreading out the shadow.
